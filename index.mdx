Agno lets you build, run, and manage your own agent platform.

Agents are becoming a core part of everyday product and operations. Workloads that were previously tedious and time-consuming are now one agent away. Agno lets you run everything in one place.

From data labeling and document processing to product copilots, employee assistants, and agent-driven software. Agno's AgentOS productionizes agents built with any framework, any model, on any cloud.

## Agno Agent Platform

1. Build agents using any framework or the Agno SDK.
2. Run agents as a service with tracing, scheduling, RBAC, and audit trails.
3. Manage everything using a unified control plane.

Your platform runs in your cloud. Your data stays in your database. You log and store sessions, memory, and traces in your own database, then use that data to auto-improve your agents.

**Here's how teams are using Agno:**

- **Product teams** use Agno to build in-product agents and chat copilots. Many power their entire product using agents running on AgentOS.
- **ML teams** use Agno to label text, image, audio and video data. Agno is natively typesafe and multi-modal, making it a natural fit for data labeling, extraction and classification.
- **AI teams** use Agno to generate synthetic data and preference pairs for training and evals. Automating document processing, knowledge organization, and eval generation workloads using Agno.
- **Data Science teams** use Agno for data enrichment, segmentation, and training data curation.
- **Data Engineering teams** are automating data quality audits, failure log analysis and weekly reports.

Everything runs on your infrastructure, managed through a beautiful UI.
